# Bharat Forge Limited – Company One-Pager

## Company Overview

Bharat Forge Limited (BFL) is a Pune-based Indian multinational and technology-driven global leader in providing high-performance, innovative safety-critical components and solutions across multiple sectors including automotive, power, oil and gas, construction & mining, rail, marine, defence, and aerospace [Source: https://www.linkedin.com/company/bharat-forge-ltd] [Confidence: High]. The company is part of the USD 3.5 billion Kalyani Group and has over half a century of manufacturing history [Source: https://www.bharatforge.com] [Confidence: High]. BFL operates with a global manufacturing footprint spanning five countries including Germany, Sweden, France, the United States, and India, with headquarters in Pune, Maharashtra, India [Source: https://www.globaldata.com/company-profile/bharat-forge-ltd] [Confidence: High].

The company holds the largest repository of metallurgical knowledge in the region and offers full-service supply capability to its geographically dispersed marquee customers from concept to product design, engineering, manufacturing, testing, and validation [Source: https://www.linkedin.com/company/bharat-forge-ltd] [Confidence: High]. Bharat Forge is positioned for exponential growth with more than 30 years of exporting experience and is expanding into capital goods and infrastructure, contributing to India's transformation into a global economic powerhouse [Source: https://www.bharatforge.com] [Confidence: High]. The company is entering the next phase of growth through its BFL 2.0 journey, focusing on expanding scale across its diversified portfolio [Source: https://leadiq.com/c/bharat-forge-ltd/5a1d7d952400002400571b3b] [Confidence: High].

Recent strategic developments include Bharat Forge becoming the first Indian company to secure a long-term contract with Embraer for manufacturing of landing gear components [Source: https://www.linkedin.com/company/bharat-forge-ltd] [Confidence: High]. Additionally, through its wholly-owned subsidiary Kalyani Powertrain Ltd, the company has partnered with APSRTC to convert veteran buses into 100% electric, zero-emission "Super Luxury" AC buses with 230+ km range, demonstrating indigenous engineering capabilities in electric mobility [Source: https://www.linkedin.com/company/bharat-forge-ltd] [Confidence: High]. The company has also achieved Stage 2 audit certification for 5S Workplace Organization at its Baramati plant, conducted by TUV India Pvt. Ltd., reflecting strong focus on workplace discipline, standardization, and operational excellence [Source: https://www.linkedin.com/company/bharat-forge-ltd] [Confidence: High].
