# Bharat Forge Limited — Company One-Pager

## Company Overview

Bharat Forge Limited (BFL) is a Pune-based Indian multinational and technology-driven global leader in manufacturing high-performance, safety-critical forged and machined components. Part of the USD 3.5 billion Kalyani Group, the company has over 50 years of manufacturing excellence and more than 30 years of exporting experience. [Source: https://www.bharatforge.com] [Confidence: High]

BFL operates 18 manufacturing locations across 5 countries (India, Germany, Sweden, France, and the United States) with 11 plants in India, and has 27 subsidiaries (14 Indian, 13 global) as of March 31, 2025. The company has an annual forging capacity of 773,910 tonnes per annum. [Source: https://www.marklines.com/en/top500/bharat-forge] [Confidence: High]

The company serves multiple sectors including automotive, railways, defence, construction & mining, aerospace, marine, and oil & gas. BFL is the world's leading powertrain and chassis component manufacturer, offering end-to-end solutions from concept to product design, engineering, manufacturing, testing, and validation, with the largest repository of metallurgical knowledge in the region. [Source: https://www.bharatforge.com] [Confidence: High]

BFL is listed on the Bombay Stock Exchange, Pune Stock Exchange, and National Stock Exchange of India (ticker: BHARATFORG.NS), with a market capitalization of over INR 592.92 billion as of March 31, 2025. [Source: https://www.marklines.com/en/top500/bharat-forge] [Confidence: High]

The company is entering its "BFL 2.0" growth phase, focusing on expanding scale, increasing automation, using digital technologies, and strengthening engineering capabilities to support the Aatmanirbhar Bharat vision and contribute to Viksit Bharat 2047. [Source: https://www.linkedin.com/company/bharat-forge-ltd] [Confidence: High]
