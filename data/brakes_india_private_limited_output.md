# Company One-Pager: Brakes India Private Limited

## Company Overview

**Brakes India Private Limited** is a leading automotive components manufacturer specializing in braking systems and ferrous castings, founded on **November 9, 1962**, and headquartered in **Padi, Chennai, Tamil Nadu, India**. The company operates as part of the **T.S. Santhanam Family (TSF) Group** and is recognized as **India's largest brake manufacturer**. [Source: https://www.tofler.in/brakes-india-private-limited/company/U35999TN1962PTC004928] [Confidence: High] [Source: https://chemdmart.com/company-profile/brakes-india-pvt-ltd] [Confidence: High]

The company operates through **three main divisions**: the **Brake Division** (producing calipers, actuation systems, drum brakes, disc brakes, and ABS systems for passenger and commercial vehicles), the **Foundry Division** (specializing in ferrous castings with capacity exceeding 200,000 tons annually), and the **Polymer Division** (focusing on polymer-based automotive components). [Source: https://chemdmart.com/company-profile/brakes-india-pvt-ltd] [Confidence: High] [Source: https://in.linkedin.com/company/brakes-india] [Confidence: High]

Brakes India maintains a **dominant market position**, supplying brakes for **3 out of 4 buses and trucks in India**, with castings reaching virtually every OEM in the country. [Source: https://www.linkedin.com/posts/siva-nagarajan_brakesindia-tvsgroup-tatamotors-activity-7362598769869127682-Z6E0] [Confidence: High] The company operates **19 manufacturing facilities** across seven Indian states (Tamil Nadu, Karnataka, Uttarakhand, Gujarat, Haryana, Andhra Pradesh, and Jharkhand), plus international operations in **Oman** (through subsidiary Dunes Oman LLC) and **Indonesia** (through a contracted plant). [Source: https://www.brakesindia.com/wp-content/uploads/2024/08/Sustainability-Report.pdf] [Confidence: High]

The company employs **over 10,000 people** (5,174 India-based employees as of one report, with total workforce including international operations exceeding 10,000). [Source: https://www.greatplacetowork.in/great/company/brakes-india-private-limited] [Confidence: High] [Source: https://tracxn.com/d/legal-entities/india/brakes-india-private-limited/__MRAQ1il_N6qb2gKxiTAE8X55WTOotHcYmN_2JjtVCRc] [Confidence: Medium]

The company's **authorized share capital** is **INR 125.00 crore** with **paid-up capital of INR 17.46 crore**. The Corporate Identification Number (CIN) is **U35999TN1962PTC004928**, and the company is classified as an **unlisted private limited company** with current status **Active**. [Source: https://www.tofler.in/brakes-india-private-limited/company/U35999TN1962PTC004928] [Confidence: High]

Brakes India has received prestigious awards including the **Deming Application Prize** (2003) and was among the first certified **green casting foundries** globally. The company holds **Greenco Platinum Award** (2018) and multiple quality and sustainability recognitions. This was partially offset by healthy aftermarket demand, with the brakes division growing **2%** on aftermarket strength. ## Select Products

**Brake Division Products:**
- **Foundation Brakes**: Complete disc brakes, drum brakes, calipers (including lightweight designs for EVs)
- **Actuation Systems**: Brake boosters, master cylinders, wheel cylinders, integrated electro-hydraulic brake systems
- **Advanced Systems**: ABS (Anti-lock Braking System), ESC (Electronic Stability Control)-ready solutions, brake-by-wire actuators
- **Hydraulic & Pneumatic Systems**: Complete hydraulic and pneumatic braking solutions for commercial vehicles
- **EV-Specific Products**: Integrated electro-hydraulic brake systems for regenerative braking, electric boosters, lightweight caliper designs optimized for electric vehicle platforms
- **Specialty Products**: 410 S-cam brake (newly developed), 430mm heavy-duty clutch system
